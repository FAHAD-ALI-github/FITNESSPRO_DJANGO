Gym Management System: Detailed Requirements
1. Project Name
fitnesspro

2. Actors of the System

Admin

Manages system-wide operations, including user accounts, memberships, revenue, and approval of new member registrations.

Trainer

Manages workout and diet plans, and assigns them to members.

Member

The end user who uses the system to view their personal profile, track their progress, and follow assigned workout and diet plans.

3. Functional Requirements
User Management
FR-1.1: The system shall support three types of users: Admin, Trainer, and Member.

FR-1.2: All users must be able to securely create an account and log in.

FR-1.3: Members shall be able to update their personal information (age, email, phone number) on their profile.

FR-1.4: Members shall be able to add their weight and height to automatically calculate and display their BMI.

Membership & Registration
FR-2.1: The system shall offer two membership types: monthly and yearly.

FR-2.2: During registration, a new member must provide their full name, age, email, phone number, username, and password.

FR-2.3: In the final step of registration, the member must upload a clear picture of their payment proof.

FR-2.4: A newly registered member shall be granted limited access to their profile immediately, with a prominent warning message that their account is pending payment approval. This warning must be removed upon admin approval.

FR-2.5: The Admin shall receive a request to approve or reject a new member's payment proof.

FR-2.6: The Admin shall have the ability to approve or reject the new member's registration based on the payment proof.

Workout Plan Management
FR-3.1: Trainers shall be able to create a library of individual exercises with details like name, description, and target muscle group.

FR-3.2: Trainers shall be able to create customizable workout plans by combining exercises from their library.

FR-3.3: Trainers can assign these custom workout plans to their members.

FR-3.4: The system shall contain a list of default/pre-made workout plans that trainers can also assign to members.

FR-3.5: Members shall be able to view their assigned workout plan and track their progress by logging completed sets, reps, and weight.

Diet Plan Management
FR-4.1: Trainers shall be able to create a library of customizable diet plans.

FR-4.2: Trainers can assign these diet plans to their members.

FR-4.3: The system shall contain a list of default/pre-made diet plans that trainers can also assign to members.

FR-4.4: Members shall be able to view their assigned diet plan and mark their progress.

Admin Portal
FR-5.1: The Admin shall have a dashboard to manage all members.

FR-5.2: The Admin can view, search, and delete member accounts.

FR-5.3: The Admin can view a member's profile with limited personal information.

FR-5.4: The Admin shall have a dedicated section for new member payment approval requests.

FR-5.5: The Admin dashboard can optionally display key analytics such as total revenue and the number of active members.

4. Core Features by User Role
Member Portal
Profile: A dedicated page to view and edit personal information (name, age, email, phone number) and to input height and weight for BMI calculation.

Workout Plan: A view to see the assigned workout plan and log daily progress.

Diet Plan: A view to see the assigned diet plan.

Trainer Portal
Workout Library: A section to create, view, edit, and delete exercises and workout plans.

Diet Plan Library: A section to create, view, edit, and delete diet plans.

Member Management: A page to view all assigned members and assign new workout and diet plans.

Admin Portal
User Management: A master list of all members with search, view, and delete capabilities.

Approval Queue: A dedicated page to view and act on pending member payment approvals.

Analytics: A dashboard displaying key business metrics (optional).

5. Basic Technical Stack
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Django's built-in templating with JavaScript for interactivity).

Backend: Django (Python).

Database: PostgreSQL (SQL) or SQLite (for local development).
